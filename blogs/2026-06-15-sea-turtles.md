NOAA educational story describing sea turtles as air-breathing reptiles with streamlined bodies and large flippers adapted for life in tropical and subtropical waters worldwide. Of seven sea turtle species globally, six inhabit U.S. waters: green, hawksbill, Kemp's ridley, leatherback, loggerhead, and olive ridley.
